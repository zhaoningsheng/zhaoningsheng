I am a PhD student in Information & Systems Engineering at Concordia University. I am supervised by Professor Jiayuan Yu. My research interest lies in Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Graphical models and Statistical Modelling. I have a passion for building new models and algorithms. In the past one year, I was focusing on the research in a brand new bootstrap kernel mixture model for unsupervised learning, and an upgraded tree Boosting Monte Carlo algorithm for reinforcement learning. From this year, I start my research in explainable machine learning.
